Platform overview

Sri Lanka's event ticketing and invitation website

VibePass.lk connects organizers, customers, invitation guests, scanners, and super admins through one trusted digital event ecosystem.

What VibePass does

The platform combines event ticket selling, seat booking, QR ticket management, digital invitation creation, RSVP collection, promotion tools, revenue management, and withdrawals.

Who it serves

  • Super admins who control platform reviews, finance, settings, fraud monitoring, and content.
  • Organizers who create events, sell tickets, manage withdrawals, and scan QR tickets.
  • Customers who buy tickets, download QR passes, create invitations, and manage notifications.
  • Guests who open invitation links, RSVP, view maps, and share events.
