Terms of service

Organizer rules, platform rights, refund eligibility, and content restrictions.

Organizer terms

Organizers must upload valid identity documents, provide real bank accounts, avoid fake ticket sales, and follow Sri Lankan laws.

Platform rights

VibePass can suspend accounts, reject events, freeze suspicious withdrawals, remove illegal content, and investigate suspicious payment or QR activity.

Refund policy

Refunds may be approved for cancelled events, duplicate payments, fraud detection, or platform issues. Customer no-shows, late arrivals, and customer mistakes are non-refundable.

Content restrictions

  • Illegal events are not allowed.
  • Gambling, hate speech, illegal adult-only content, and fraudulent ticket sales are not allowed.
  • Events can be rejected or sent back for corrections during review.
